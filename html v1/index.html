Goal

Managers, users, or faculties can get analyzed information with interactive charts for obtaining revenue and profit and reducing cost. Reporters or students can practice what they studied last semester and test what they will learn in winter semester.

Scope

* Deliver additional information on the previous report.
* Give revenue information about a specific month with hover functionality.
* Give detailed information by categories such as cities, stores, and products.
* Give mobility that users can see on the web.
